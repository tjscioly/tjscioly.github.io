Our Achievements

Since the establishment of our team in 2009, TJSO has won ten consecutive regional tournaments and four Virginia state tournaments. During the 2019 season, TJSO broke the record for the best overall score in Virginia state tournament history and shattered our own record for the best-ever national placement by a Virginia high school.

Below are some other achievements from recent years:

  • 2019-2020
    • Carnegie Mellon University Invitational: all 3 teams top-6
    • University of Mary Washington Regional: 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
    • University of Pennsylvania Invitational (SOUP): 4th place
      • Chemistry Lab, 6th place
      • Circuit Lab, 5th place
      • Codebusters, 5th place
      • Disease Detectives, 1st place
      • Experimental Design, 1st place
      • Geologic Mapping, 5th place
      • Solar Power, 2nd place
      • Sounds of Music, 3rd place
      • Wright Stuff, 1st place
  • 2018-2019
    • Duke University Invitational: all 3 teams top-6
    • Charlottesville Regional: 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
    • Cornell University Invitational: 4th place
    • Virginia State: 1st place
    • National: 12th place
      • Circuit Lab, 1st place
      • Codebusters, 5th place
      • Environmental Chemistry, 3rd place
      • Fossils, 4th place
      • Mission Possible, 6th place
      • Sounds of Music, 5th place
      • Thermodynamics, 2nd place
  • 2017-2018
    • Virginia Tech Invitational: 1st place
    • Fairfax Invitational: all 3 teams top-4
    • Charlottesville Regional: 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
    • Virginia State: 1st place
    • National: 26th place
      • Codebusters, 1st place
      • Disease Detectives, 1st place
      • Dynamic Planet, 1st place
