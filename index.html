Welcome to TJSO!

About Us

Who We Are

Thomas Jefferson Science Olympiad (TJSO) is a nationally competitive high-school organization, composed of three 15-person teams.

What We Do

Events

Each of our teams compete in 23 events, with topics ranging from forensic science and aquatic environments to compound machines and cryptography. Every team member gets to do 3-4 events.

Competition

Each year, TJSO participates in invitational, regional, and state tournaments. If we win the state tournament (as we have done in previous years), TJSO moves on to nationals!

Teamwork

TJSO is built on strong teamwork. Each event is done with at least one partner, requiring close cooperation between teammates. Furthermore, our community strives to support and uplift each individual.

Want to learn more about our events? Read the official rulebook!

Our Achievements

Since the establishment of our team in 2009, TJSO has won ten consecutive regional tournaments and four Virginia state tournaments. During the 2019 season, TJSO broke the record for the best overall score in Virginia state tournament history and shattered our own record for the best-ever national placement by a Virginia high school.
